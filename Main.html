Modules & Topics Covered

Duration: 4 weeks | Credits: 3

Fundamentals of IT, career paths, industry overview, ethical considerations in technology, and the role of IT in modern organizations.

Duration: 8 weeks | Credits: 4

Core programming concepts: variables, loops, functions, OOP principles. Languages covered: Python, Java, and C++. Hands-on coding exercises and projects.

Duration: 8 weeks | Credits: 4

Arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs, sorting algorithms, searching techniques. Algorithm analysis, complexity theory, and optimization strategies.

Duration: 8 weeks | Credits: 4

SQL fundamentals, relational databases, NoSQL databases, data modeling, query optimization. Hands-on experience with MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.

Duration: 8 weeks | Credits: 4

OSI model, TCP/IP protocols, routing, switching, network security, wireless networks. Cisco networking concepts and hands-on lab simulations.

Duration: 12 weeks | Credits: 6

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Vue.js. Backend: Node.js, Express, Django. Mobile: React Native, Flutter. Full-stack project development.

Duration: 8 weeks | Credits: 4

Encryption, authentication, network security, ethical hacking, penetration testing, security compliance, and risk management frameworks.

Duration: 8 weeks | Credits: 4

SDLC methodologies, requirements gathering, system design patterns, UML modeling, project planning, and software testing strategies.

Duration: 16 weeks | Credits: 8

Comprehensive real-world project integrating all learned skills. Team collaboration, client interaction, documentation, presentation, and deployment of a complete IT solution.
